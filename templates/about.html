About this Project

In many educational institutions, the traditional methods of recording and managing student grades are often inefficient, prone to errors, and lack real-time accessibility. Teachers manually input grades into spreadsheets, leading to difficulties in tracking student performance, delayed feedback, and potential miscalculations. Additionally, students and parents have limited visibility into academic progress, which can hinder communication and support.

The objective of this mini-project is to develop a Student Grade Management System that automates grade entry, provides real-time grade calculations, and offers intuitive data visualization.
