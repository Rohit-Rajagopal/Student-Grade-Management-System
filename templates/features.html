Features

  • For Teachers:
    1. Search for a particular student but usn to check their grades.
    2. Easily add, edit and delete grades.
    3. Check students' report cards to check their SGPAs or their overall CGPA.
  • For Students:
    1. Check your grades of all semesters as well as any remarks from the teacher.
    2. A report card with SGPAs of all semesters as well as overall CGPA and any backlogs.
